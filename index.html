ABERYSTWYTH EXTINCTION REBELLION "THE BIG ONE"

Extinction Rebellion have announced that they are aiming to bring together all of the UK's environmentalist groups and the general public to get 100,000 people to meet up in non-obstructive protest at parliment square on April 21st.

We at Aber XR have organised a coach to take 70 people to london and are offering out seats to any individuals interested in participating.

You dont have to be part of Extinction Rebellion to join, anyone interested in attending the event is welcome!

If you want to try get into environmentalism or just feel like you want to try help fix things, this is a really nice way to start!
