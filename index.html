ABERYSTWYTH EXTINCTION REBELLION "THE BIG ONE"

Extinction Rebellion have announced that they are aiming to bring together all of the UK's general public to get 100,000 people to meet up in non-obstructive protest at parliment square on April 21st.

We at Aber XR have organised a coach to take 70 people to london and are offering out seats to anyone interested in participating.

If you want to try get into activism, are worried about the environment or just feel like you want to try help fix things, this is a really nice way to start!

You dont have to be part of Extinction Rebellion to join, anyone interested in attending the event is welcome! We will get the bus to London on the morning of the 21st and head back the same day, though you are welcome to stay longer and make your own way back :)
